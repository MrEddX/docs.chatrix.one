    Welcome¶

    Hello and welcome to Chatrix.One! A Bulgarian XMPP server for free, end-to-end encrypted communication, without telemetry and no ads. Freely send messages, emojis, pictures, files and locations. Enjoy quality voice and video calls in real time. Wishing you pleasant moments with your family, friends and loved ones!

    Features¶

    • Instant messages with reactions and emoji support;
    • Share images and files of different types;
    • Location sharing;
    • Audio and video calls;
    • Voice messages;
    • Group chat;
    • Indications for sent, delivered and read message;
    • Photos (Avatars) for your contacts;
    • Synchronization with desktop;
    • Compatibility with the most used operating systems.

    Why Chatrix.One?¶

    • Uses proven XMPP technology;
    • Provides End-to-end encryption with OMEMO or OpenPGP;1
    • Encrypted audio and video calls (DTLS-SRTP);
    • RESPECTS your privacy;2
    • DOES NOT require personal information upon registration;
    • DOES NOT serve to you advertisements, banners and offers;
    • DOES NOT require consent and acceptance of insane policy3;
    • Uses Ejabberd, an open source platform4;
    • 100% compatibility with the available XEPs;
    • Decentralized architecture;
    • Server located in Switzerland - accessible from all over the world;
    • Powered by 100% renewable energy!
